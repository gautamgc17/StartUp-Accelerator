About US

StartUp Accelerator provides an integrated dashboard for sharing of innovation and startups with success stories across Higher Educational Institutions and relevant stakeholders. We aim to provide a single platform for the students/ startup fellows/ entrepreneurs to register and submit their their ideas / prototypes / business idea. Apart from, facilitating the various agencies (Govt. / Pvt.) to register and submit their problem statements, we also provide a platform for Venture capitalist / Angel investors for analyzing ideas / prototypes / solutions of their interest to invest fund.
